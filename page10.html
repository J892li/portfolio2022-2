FBS but looks so sweet?

That is correct. Andrometa is a  robot shooting game. Players need to find the exit of the room in a limited time, collect enough candy and then shoot the enemy to get more points!

core mechanics

How the game work 

The core mechanics of this game are shooting, dodging enemies, leveling up after shooting enemies, and continuing the game.

Key Features

Challenge 1 - Game Pivot

This project was originally designed to be a hardcore robot shooter game. The product is trying to build a thrilling and gory experience.
The team decided to change the experience from traditional shooter games and replace it with a contrasting and cute style during the last two month. We changed from machine guns to bubble guns & bubble gum, resulting in a very tight schedule.
The solution is, as a PM , I am here to facilitate and support. We carefully discussed whether we need to change game direction as a team, and the answer is Yes. Our UIUX team and 3D artists worked very hard and caught up with time to successfully complete the project in the end.

Challenge 2 Art re-deisgn

Sifi - Robot - to cubic Robot is not easy! 
Our team stopped thinking about the concept art for the previous deisgn and started to draft cute robot for better game experiences. And finally made it in 1 and half month from concept to model. 

Solution ： Active listening and trying to coordinate the dependency between art and tech team. 

Took a lot of effort and thanks to our artists in the team! 

Challenge 3 From PC to Mobile

The final problem is: The original design was designed for PC games. The player should use the keyboard to move the character. After we pivot the game style, we found that the character's settings and weapons were not suitable for use on a computer screen.

Solution: We decided to switch to mobile design. We updated the prototype and related GUI design for mobile users. And ideally , in the future, we might expand to PC again.
